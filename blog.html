An economy platform built on social, commerce and business to inherently amplify anyone's ability to earn a steady income that is cheap to operate, economically viable, sustainable, and accessible to anyone with a smartphone. On Wednesday the 14th of December 2022, in the bustling city of Lagos Nigeria.



According to the Bureau of Statistics, Nigeria unemployment rate is at 33.3% and in Africa, over 500 million people between the ages of 15 - 45 years do not have a sustainable income, due to economic recessions, high inflation rate, among others. In Nigeria, the minimum wage is 25% lower than the price of essentials goods. It is predicted that the Africa's population is expected to grow to 2.5 billion people by 2050 with Nigeria being the center of this growth as compared to the 1.4 billion people presently and if sustainable solutions and opportunities are not created now, poverty will tremendously increase. Hence, the introduction of 2GEDA.



An adage in Yoruba would say “Ise logun ise”, this means, work is the medicine for poverty. https://www.2geda.ng is the medicine. We are an organized trading marketplace that employs and pays its users to facilitate the buying and selling of groceries and produce via a smartphone.



Our mission is to connect humanity with the resources that inspires them for fulfillment and our objectives for this prototype launch are to recruit and onboard employees, develop database for customers and amplify the solutions awareness.
